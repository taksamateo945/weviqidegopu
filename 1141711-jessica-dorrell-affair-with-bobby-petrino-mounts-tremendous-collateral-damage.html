Jessica Dorrell: Affair with Bobby Petrino Mounts Tremendous Collateral Damage | News, Scores, Highl

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 12, 2024
Photo Credit: ABC News

Bobby Petrino has been fired from his post as head coach of the Arkansas Razorbacks football team, but that is not nearly the end of it. 

The fallout from a secret affair continues to mount, and those affected must continue to fix lives that have been irreparably shattered. 

It's important to note that we still do not know the entire story of what went transpired between Petrino and Jessica Dorrell, but the aftermath fills in some of the missing pieces. 

Petrino has since apologized for what he calls an indecent relationship, via ESPN. 

The simplest response I have is: I'm sorry. These two words seem very inadequate. But that is my heart....I chose to engage in an improper relationship. I also made several poor decisions following the end of that relationship and in the aftermath of the accident. I accept full responsibility for what has happened.

What of that relationship? The same ESPN report issues that Dorrell, a former Arkansas volleyball player who was hired to work for the Razorbacks Foundation, was said to "at one time" be engaged to Josh Morgan, the athletic department's director of swimming and diving operations. 

Photo Credit: IBTimes

ESPN received the news from a source close to the situation, and it was believed that Morgan was still employed at the time of the report. 

That seems to have changed. 

We do not, as of yet, know when the two were engaged, or when that ceased being the case. What we do know is that Morgan is no longer listed on the campus' website. 

Petrino is not the only man to have lost his job in the awful story, because Morgan may very well have moved on. 

The swimming news site, Swim Swam reports on Morgan and his position with the university. They issue that he is no longer listed on the colleges site, and is no longer listed as his most recent position as strength and conditioning coach.

A selfish decision by Petrino and Dorrell have cost them both their reputations and standing in the community, a potential career for Morgan and has caused a black eye for the program.

What's more, Petrino is married with four kids.

The scandal is not only ugly, but far reaching.
