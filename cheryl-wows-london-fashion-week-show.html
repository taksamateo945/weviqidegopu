Cheryl Wows At London Fashion Week Show

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 22, 2024

Cheryl stepped out tonight for her first public appearance this year to support her friend Gareth Pugh at London Fashion Week.

The X-Factor judge arrived wearing a slim fitting, grey pencil dress paired alongside a pair of strappy heels and a clutch bag. Despite recent claims of her divorce with Jean Bernard Fernandez-Versini she couldn’t look happier tonight as she arrived.

Her outfit complemented her slim figure as she posed at the top of the staircase.

Posting a picture on Instagram she showed off her flawless curls and make-up as she prepared for the night ahead.

Even in the midst of divorce it’s great to see Cheryl coming out beaming, and we’re happy to see her happy!
