Organza Bead Bag Bomboniera Blessed Celebration

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

SKU BOM-BAG-WD-220

Share this:

Delicate beaded organza bag filled with 7 Jordan almonds and accented with a bow at top. Two delicate satin streamer ribbons and flower centerpiece complement the look. The perfect Bomboniera for weddings, christenings, any other special event.

***

Size of bag 5" x 4" without beads, 5" x 5" with beads.

***

Color bag choice applies to the organza bag. 

The large bow is always white.

The streamer color applies to the two thin satin ribbons.

***

There is a 20 count order minimum for favors made with personalized ribbons. Personalized orders less than 20 are subject to a one-time $5 printer set up fee.

***

Our design team is dedicated to providing our customers with unique and elegant bombonieres. Each one of our premium quality favors is handmade with love and care for your special event. We are honored that our creations can accompany your family to your most treasured moments.
