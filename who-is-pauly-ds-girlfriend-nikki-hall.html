Who is Pauly D's girlfriend Nikki Hall?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, February 1, 2024

PAULY D has made his name as a reality TV star and a DJ.

Similarly, his girlfriend Nikki has made a name for herself as a TV personality and actress.

Who is Pauly D’s girlfriend Nikki Hall?

Nikki Hall was born on April 15, 1992, in Los Angeles, California.

Nikki is an actress, model, and reality TV star.

She is the brand model for some clothing brands such as Icon Swimwear and Bamboo Underwear.

She rose to fame when she appeared on the reality TV reality show, A Double Shot at Love, being the girlfriend of DJ and Television personality, Pauly D.

Who is Pauly D?

Paul Michael DelVecchio Jr., popularly known as Pauly D., was born on July 5, 1980 (age 42 years), in Providence, Rhode Island.

Pauly D. is a DJ and television personality.

He is best known for being a cast member of MTV’s reality show Jersey Shore.

Furthermore, he made a three-album deal with 50 Cent’s G-Unit Records and G-Note Records in 2011.

Pauly is also the first of the Jersey Shore cast to get his own spin-off show, The Pauly D Project.

What is Pauly D’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Pauly D has an estimated net worth of approximately $20million, as of July 2022.

His main source of wealth is said to be from his career as a DJ and TV personality.

He also earns income from brand endorsements and sponsorships.
