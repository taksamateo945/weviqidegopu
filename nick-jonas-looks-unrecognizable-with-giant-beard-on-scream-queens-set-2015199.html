Nick Jonas Looks Unrecognizable With Giant Beard on Scream Queens Set

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Talk about a master of disguise! Nick Jonas was barely recognizable with a giant beard and a huge wig on the set of Scream Queens in New Orleans on Thursday, Sept. 17.

The 23-year-old crooner, who is usually relatively clean-cut, looked 10 years older and several decades out of place as he talked on a cell phone dressed in a blue-and-green striped polo shirt, puffy green vest, and belted khaki pants.

Jonas will play a preppy frat boy named Boone on the upcoming Fox series, which will also star Jamie Lee Curtis, Keke Palmer, and Styler Samuels.

Earlier that day, the “Levels” singer was spotted onset with his typical buzz cut, a blue-and-navy collegiate sweater, and dark slacks.
