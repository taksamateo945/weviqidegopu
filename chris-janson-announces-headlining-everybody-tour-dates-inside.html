Chris Janson Announces Headlining Everybody Tour Dates Inside Celeb Secrets Country

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Chris Janson is hitting the road this fall for a special tour!

Inspired by his brand new album due September 22 and available now for pre-order, the Everybody Tour includes headlining dates in addition to fairs and festivals all across the nation.

“I’ve spent the year touring with some of the best country artists in the game, and every single show gets me more excited than the last,” said Janson. “I’m ready to take it to the next level for my biggest fans with my own headlining dates on The EVERYBODY Tour. It’s going to be a party.”

Janson is kicking off the run tonight in Anderson, South Carolina at Denver Downs Farms (full list of dates below). The tour will continue into 2018, featuring a performance at the legendary Ryman Auditorium in February. Pre-sale tickets for the Nashville show will be available for fans via email starting Wednesday, September 13 at 10:00 a.m. and through the Ryman / local radio starting Thursday, September 14 at 10:00 a.m. Fans can sign up for ticket pre-sale offers and more by joining Janson’s email list at www.ChrisJanson.com. Tickets will go on sale to the general public the following day,September 15.

Check out the full list of tour dates below…
