Who is Glenda Hatchett:

Glenda Hatchett is a famous Lawyer. She was born on May 31, 1951 and her birthplace is Atlanta, GA. Glenda is also well known as, Lawyer, TV judge, and author known for hosting the show Judge Hatchett, which ran in the 2000s. She also founded The Hatchett Firm, PC in 2014.

Glenda is originated from United States. She spoke at the 2013 Pennsylvania Conference for Women, where Hillary Clinton was the closing speaker.
