Cameron Diaz Bio, Height, Age

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 22, 2024

Who is Cameron Diaz?

Cameron Diaz is an American actress, comedian, producer, and former fashion model. Due to her beauty, good figure, and nice face she started working in the model industry. While in high school, she signed a modeling contract with Elite Model Management. She got her big break with her debut film at 21 in front of Jim Carrey in the comedy The Mask (1994).

She is also known for her roles in films like My Best Friend`s Wedding, There`s Something About Mary, Charlie`s Angels, and Vanilla Sky. Besides that, she has given her voice in the Shrek series.

Short bio (Age, Parents, Siblings)

Cameron Michelle Diaz was born on the 30th of August 1972, in San Diego, California, United States of America. This beautiful actress is the daughter of Emilio Diaz, a second-generation Cuban-American oil company foreman, and his wife Billie, who is of Native American, Italian, and German descent. She has two older siblings, Chimene and Michael Diaz.

She attended Los Cerritos Elementary School and graduated from Long Beach Polytechnic High School, Long Beach, California, the USA in 1990.

How tall is Cameron Diaz? (Height, Weight)

Cameron Diaz’s height is 5’9” or 174 cm and weighs about 58 kg. Furthermore, she has a measurement of 34-23-35 inches respectively. With her enchanting looks, blue eyes, and blonde hair, Diaz won the hearts of millions. Similarly, her shoe is 9 (US) and her dress size is 4 (US).

How much is Cameron Diaz Net Worth?

Cameron Diaz’s net worth is around $ 150 million. In 2010, Forbes listed her as one of the wealthiest female celebrities.
