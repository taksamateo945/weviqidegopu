Big Meech Wife, Son, Sister, Net Worth, Brother, Dad

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Demetrius “Big Meech” Flenory is one of the most notorious drug kingpins in American history.

He co-founded the Black Mafia Family (BMF), a criminal organization that made over $270 million from cocaine trafficking and money laundering in the 1990s and 2000s.

He was also involved in the hip-hop music industry, promoting artists like Young Jeezy and Bleu DaVinci through his BMF Entertainment label.

But who is the woman behind this infamous figure? Does Big Meech have a wife or a girlfriend? Here is what we know about his love life.

How old is Big Meech?

Big Meech was born on June 21, 1968, in Detroit, Michigan.

He is currently 55 years old as of 2023.

He dropped out of high school and started selling drugs with his younger brother Terry “Southwest T” Flenory, who was also his partner in crime.

They moved to Atlanta, Georgia, in the early 1990s and expanded their drug empire across the country.

Big Meech wife

According to public records, Big Meech does not have a wife and has never been married in his life.

However, there have been speculations that he had a long-term girlfriend named Tonesa Welch, who claimed to be the first lady of BMF.

She even appeared in a reality show called BMF Wives, along with other women who were allegedly married or engaged to BMF members.

However, this claim was denied by BMF Entertainment CEO Tammy Cowins, who said that Big Meech and his brother Terry had never tied the knot with anyone.

She also said that the only woman who deserved the title of the first lady of BMF was their mother, Lucille Flenory.

Big Meech net worth

Big Meech was once one of the richest criminals in the world, with an estimated net worth of $100 million at his peak.

He made his fortune from his drug business and his music label, which served as a front to launder his illegal money.

He also lived a lavish lifestyle, spending millions on cars, jewelry, houses, parties, and celebrities.

However, his wealth came at a high price.

In 2005, he was arrested and indicted by the DEA for running a criminal enterprise.

In 2008, he pleaded guilty to drug trafficking and money laundering charges and was sentenced to 30 years in prison.

His assets were seized by the government and his net worth plummeted to $500 thousand as of 2023.

Big Meech kids

Big Meech has one confirmed son named Demetrius Flenory Jr., also known as Lil Meech.

He was born in Detroit and is currently 21 years old as of 2023.

He is a rapper and an actor, who plays his father’s role in the Starz series BMF, which is produced by 50 Cent.

Lil Meech has no connection to his father’s criminal past and has expressed his admiration for him as a father figure.

Big Meech sister

Big Meech has a younger sister named Nicole Flenory, who was born in 1974 in Dartmouth St., Detroit.

She is the least-known member of the Flenory family and has kept a low profile away from the spotlight.

Nicole Flenory lives in Lincoln Park, Michigan, with her family and sells BMF-branded merchandise online.

She also supports her brother’s release from prison and has shown appreciation for Laila Pruitt, who portrays her in the BMF series.

Big Meech and his brother

Big Meech and his brother Terry Flenory have been inseparable since their childhood.

They started their drug business together as teenagers and became one of the most powerful duos in the underworld.

They founded BMF in 1985 and ran it until their arrest in 2005.

However, their relationship was not always smooth. In 2001, they had a falling out over a dispute with their supplier in Los Angeles.

Terry moved to LA to head his own branch of BMF, while Big Meech stayed in Atlanta to run the main distribution center.

They also had different visions for their organization: Terry wanted to keep it low-key and focus on making money, while Big Meech wanted to make it more visible and influential in the hip-hop scene.

Big Meech release date

Big Meech was originally set to be released from prison in October 2032, after serving his 30-year sentence.

However, he received a sentence reduction of 36 months by a U.S. district judge in June 2021, based on changes in sentencing guidelines for drug cases that existed before November 1, 2014.

This means that he could be eligible for parole or release sometime around 2028, depending on his behavior and other factors.

His brother Terry was granted a compassionate release in May 2020, due to the coronavirus pandemic and his health issues.

He is currently under home confinement and has to pay $4.6 million in restitution.

Big Meech dad

Big Meech’s dad was Charles Flenory, who was a car thief and a loan shark.

He was killed in 1977 after trying to run from an FBI agent.

He left behind his wife Lucille and his three children: Demetrius, Terry, and Nicole.

Big Meech and his brother Terry followed their father’s footsteps into the criminal world, while their sister Nicole stayed away from it.

Conclusion

Big Meech is one of the most notorious drug kingpins in American history, who co-founded the Black Mafia Family with his brother Terry Flenory.

He was also involved in the hip-hop music industry, promoting artists like Young Jeezy and Bleu DaVinci through his BMF Entertainment label.

He was arrested and sentenced to 30 years in prison in 2008, but he could be released earlier due to a sentence reduction.

His mother is Lucille Flenory, who supports her son’s release from prison.
