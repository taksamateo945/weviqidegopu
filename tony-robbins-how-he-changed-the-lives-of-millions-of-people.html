Tony Robbins: How He Changed the Lives of Millions of People

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, January 31, 2024

Who is Tony Robbins?

He is not just a motivational speaker.

He is a life coach, a best-selling author, a philanthropist, and a business strategist.

Tony has helped millions of people transform their lives through his books, seminars, and personal coaching.

He has worked with celebrities, athletes, presidents, and CEOs.

He has also created several successful businesses and foundations that support various causes around the world.

Robbins is a man who lives by his own motto.

Tony Robbins net worth

He has a net worth of $600 million.

This is through a vast self-help empire that includes seminars, books, public speeches, private coaching, and more.

He is also an investor in several businesses and multiple valuable real estate properties.

priming Tony Robbins

Priming is a technique that Robbins uses to start his day with a positive and powerful state of mind.

It involves a 10-minute exercise that combines breathing, gratitude, visualization, and affirmation.

Priming helps Tony to overcome any negative emotions, focus on his goals, and align his body and mind for success.

tony robbins disc

DISC is a personality assessment tool that measures four aspects of human behavior: Dominance, Influence, Steadiness, and Conscientiousness.

Tony Robbins DISC test is a free online test that you can take to discover your dominant personality type and learn how to communicate better and achieve your goals.

Tony Robbins DISC test is based on the DISC theory developed by psychologist William Moulton Marston in the 1920s.

The test consists of 24 questions where you have to choose the word that best describes you and the word that least describes you.

The test takes about 10 minutes to complete and you can download your results right away.

The four DISC personality types are:

  • Dominance: People who score high in dominance are direct, results-oriented, decisive, competitive and strong problem solvers.
  • Influence: People who score high in influence are charming, enthusiastic, optimistic, persuasive, and inspiring.
  • Steadiness: People who score high in steadiness are understanding, team-oriented, patient, stable and sincere.
  • Conscientiousness: People who score high in conscientiousness are analytical, diplomatic, precise, compliant, and objective.
