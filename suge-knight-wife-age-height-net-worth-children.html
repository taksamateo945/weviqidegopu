Suge Knight Wife, Age, Height, Net Worth, Children

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, May 9, 2024

Suge Knight is a former American record producer, music executive, and football player who co-founded Death Row Records in 1991.

He is best known for his involvement in the careers of hip-hop stars such as Dr. Dre, Snoop Dogg, and Tupac Shakur.

He is also notorious for his legal troubles, including a 28-year prison sentence for a fatal hit-and-run in 2015.

Here is everything you need to know about Suge Knight wife, children, net worth, and more.

How old is Suge Knight

Suge Knight was born on April 19, 1965, in Compton, California.

He is currently 56 years old as of 2021.

His childhood nickname “Sugar Bear” gave rise to his stage name “Suge”, which is pronounced like “Shug”.

Suge Knight wife

Suge Knight has been married twice in his life.

His first wife was Sharitha Lee Golden, whom he married in 1989.

They met at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, where Knight played college football as a defensive end.

They had a daughter named Arion in 1993, who is the goddaughter of rapper Snoop Dogg.

However, their marriage ended in divorce after less than 10 years, allegedly due to Knight’s infidelity.

His second wife was Michel’le Denise Toussant, a famous R&B singer who was previously engaged to Dr. Dre. They married in 1999 and had a daughter named Bailei in 2002.

However, their marriage also ended in divorce in 2007, after Michel’le accused Knight of being abusive and violent towards her.

Knight also had several other children from different relationships.

Suge Knight net worth

Suge Knight’s net worth is estimated to be $200 thousand as of October 2023.

He amassed his wealth through his music executive duties, where he received a lot of royalties and profits from the success of Death Row Records and its artists.

At its peak, Death Row Records was worth over $100 million and sold over 150 million albums worldwide.

However, Knight’s fortune declined rapidly due to his legal issues, mismanagement of the label, bankruptcy filings, and lawsuits from former associates and artists.

He also lost most of his assets, including his mansion, cars, jewelry, and shares of Death Row Records.

Suge Knight height

As mentioned earlier, Suge Knight is 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 meters) tall.

He has a large and muscular build that made him a formidable presence on the football field and in the music industry.

He used his height and weight to intimidate his rivals and enemies, as well as to protect his artists and friends.

Where is Suge Knight now?

Suge Knight is currently serving a 28-year prison sentence at the Richard J. Donovan Correctional Facility in San Diego County, California.

He was sentenced in October 2018 after pleading no contest to voluntary manslaughter for killing Terry Carter and injuring Cle “Bone” Sloan with his truck in January 2015.

The incident occurred after an argument on the set of the movie Straight Outta Compton, which depicted the rise and fall of N.W.A., the rap group that included Dr. Dre and Ice Cube.

Knight claimed that he acted in self-defense and feared for his life when he ran over the two men.

Knight’s sentence was enhanced by California’s three-strikes law because he had two prior convictions for assault with a deadly weapon and robbery.

He will be eligible for parole in October 2037.
