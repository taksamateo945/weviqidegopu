Magic Emperor Chapter 380 Release Date and Where to Read It?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, April 20, 2024

In the popular Manhua series Magic Emperor, former peak cultivator Zhuo Yifan meets his end at the hands of those who were supposed to help him.

But, he is resurrected as the slothful scion of a noble family and endowed with the Magic Emperor’s power via a dusty old scroll. He plans on using his new identity and abilities to exact revenge on his tormentors and remake the world.

The story of Manhua is inspired by Nightingale’s book of the same name. Ji Le Niao Tu drew it. It has been serialized on KuaiKan Manhua since 2018, with more than 370 chapters now available. Many people enjoy reading this Manhua because of the captivating plot and fascinating characters.

Magic Emperor Chapter 380 Release Date

Currently, Magic Emperor has no set schedule, thus it is unknown when Chapter 380 will be released. If we look at the release dates of the other chapters of the Manhua, we may safely assume that Magic Emperor chapter 380 will be published on Friday, April 7, 2023.

In Review: Chapter 379 of The Magic Emperor

Yuwen Yong resented his older brother for the acclaim he received. Yuwen Wo was greatly dismayed by his younger brother’s charges that he had manipulated the public to get sympathy. He made it obvious that all of Tianyu’s clans had descended upon the big imperial city at the royal family’s invitation.

They had to wait in the cold to form a human chain before being let inside the building. Hence, as crown prince, he owed it to the people of his country to treat them with kindness. Yuwen Yong informed him it was all an act and questioned whether or not they were worthy of being invited.

Yuwen Yong claimed that they were forced to attend the imperial degree, so he couldn’t understand how the indecisive Yuwen Wo could feel sympathy for them and rise to power.

Yuwen Wo warned him that if he continued to mistreat Tianyu in this manner, he would never be able to rule fairly or win their respect. The emperor’s father didn’t make Yuwen Yong crown prince for the same reason. In terms of knowledge and methods, Yuwen Yong assured Yuwen Wo there was no difference between them.

Magic Emperor Chapter 380: Where To Read It?

Magic Emperor Chapter 380 will be published in Chinese by KuaiKan Manhua.

Several websites offer English translations of the Manhua series, including Comikey, INKR, Tapas in South Korea, and soon, TOPTOON, where you can read Magic Emperor chapter 380.
