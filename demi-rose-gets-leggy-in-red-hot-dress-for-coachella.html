Gabby Windey Is RED HOT In Tight Dress For 'Bachelorette' Finale

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 4, 2024

Gabby Windey slayed at “The Bachelorette” finale last night, leaving her fans’ jaws on the floor as she wore a tight red dress.

Gabby Windey On Her Way To ‘Bachelorette’ Finale

Gabby Windey attended “The Bachelorette” finale last night. Before making her grand entrance on stage, she shared some photos of herself in the car on her way to the live finale.

She simply captioned the post, “Omw,” which means “on my way”.

Fans and followers flooded the comments section, expressing their thoughts. “Queen Gabby ❤️‍?? so gorgeous!!!,” one fan wrote as another chimed in, “princess of bachelor nation ?‍♀️”.

Others wrote things such as “Wow you are so Gorgeous love your hair love your red dress ?❤️” and “I ❤️ a lady in red @gabby.windey”.

Fans Rally Around Gabby Windey After She Came Out

Fans are still supporting the former “Bachelorette” lead after she recently announced that she is dating a girl.

“You are gorgeous. You are loved by many. You didn’t deceive anyone in anyway so please don’t listen to the haters. We are so very happy for you,” one fan commented on the post, while another chimed in, ” talked to my mom today and she brought up how you have a gf now. I was like, she was my fave and good bc NO MAN DESERVES HER!!!!”

Another commented, “Watched, loved seeing you and your New L❤️VE, so happy for you Gabby, you deserve the BEST, Bkess you and your New adventure…. Namaste”

Earlier this month, Gabby Windey announced that she is gay, and is now dating girlfriend Robby Hoffman. “I always just want to live my truth, and my story,” Windey said on “The View”. “And so I have been seeing someone for a couple months, and I’ve been keeping it a little more private because it is like a bigger story and a bigger conversation, because I’m dating a girl!”

“The View” co-host Sunny Hostin said she “didn’t see that twist coming,” to which Windey replied with, “I don’t think anyone did. No, not even me.”

Gabby Windey Talks New Relationship

Gabby Windey also took to Instagram to share more surrounding her new relationship.

“Hi, you guys,” she began to say in her video, “Lots to say, honestly, and it’s hard to get it out all right now. But I know everyone’s really curious about who I’ve been dating, and thank you,” Windey continues. “You know I love to like, stir the pot and tease, but I did want to wait until I was ready,” she went on.

The former “Bachelorette” continued, “Because it is a larger conversation. Because I’m dating a girl,” she happily announces. “It’s been, honestly, truly, like, really the best experience over these last three months, and I’ve been in a relationship I feel like I never have before. But I wanted to tell you guys, because I always just want to live my life honestly and have you all along with me,” she said.

Vinny Guadagnino recently commented on Gabby Windey’s new relationship, saying “I found out with the rest of the world, […] but I’m just happy for her.”

He continued, “She’s a great person, great human.” The “Jersey Shore” star went on to say that the two never dated, despite having flirted on social media. “We’ve never dated, that was just a big […] you know we were both just on the show [“Dancing With the Stars”] at the same time and we left one emoji to each other, so yeah she’s a cool friend. She’s awesome so I’m happy for her.”
