Update: Britney Spears Sister Jamie Lynn Relationship

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 31, 2024

Are Britney Spears and her sister Jamie Lynn Spears on better terms?

As reported by DailyMail, Britney Spears’ mother is reportedly pleading with the singer to reconnect with her sister.

Amidst the 13-year conservatorship and the public feud between Britney Spears and her younger sister Jamie Lynn, Lynne Spears, their mother, is reportedly pleading with the 41-year-old pop star to mend their strained relationship.

The article highlights that Britney has faced difficulties within her family during her conservatorship.

However, since reestablishing a close bond with her mother, Lynne Spears, an insider revealed that Lynne is now making efforts to mend the relationship between her daughters.

Britney Spears and Jamie Lynn Spears

According to the insider’s information, Lynne Spears is allegedly imploring Britney to reconcile with her sister, Jamie Lynn, and both parties are leaning towards a positive response.

Lynne acknowledges that Britney misses her sister, and she believes the feeling is mutual.

The source also reveals that Britney still holds the belief that Jamie Lynn is the one who caused the rift, and if they are to meet, it would require Jamie Lynn to reach out first, rather than the other way around.

The insider further discloses that Lynne is currently strategizing on how to facilitate this reunion and is determined to ensure that everything is resolved perfectly.

Another source sheds light on Lynne’s efforts to bring the sisters together, stating that she recognizes the need for time to mend their relationship and restore harmony.

Lynne’s love for Britney is immense, and she has been striving for a long time to heal the wounds of the past.

Please note that the information provided is based on statements from anonymous insider sources and should be treated as such until confirmed by the involved parties.
