Erika Christensen, Cole Maness Share Wedding Photo: See Her Dress!

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 29, 2024

UPDATE: New photos of Christensen’s dress have been revealed! The full-length pics show the Parenthood alum’s gorgeous gown — a sleeveless design with all-over netting and a short train — in all its bridal glory. Christensen carried a bouquet of white and purple flowers, and wore her blonde hair in a high bun.

Other pics show her posing with her new husband. In one, the two kneel close to the ground and share a kiss under the bride’s veil.

—

Looking lovely! Erika Christensen‘s new husband, Cole Maness, shared a stunning photograph of him and his new wife via Instagram on Sunday, Sept. 6, a day after the happy couple said, “I do.”

The 33-year-old actress looks beautiful in a modern-looking, sleeveless white gown, her hair pulled back in a bun, and framed with a simple, pretty, headress made of white flowers and green foliage. Her cyclist groom is wearing a blue suit, his bowtie untied but still hanging around his neck, and both are gazing into the distance, looking super cool!

The ceremony was held in California, in a rustic setting with minimal decor, under an ancient oak tree. “They seemed giddy and madly in love,” one source told Us of the happy couple.

Christensen — a member of the Church of Scientology — has been relatively quiet about the relationship, but does document their adventures together via Instagram. When not on set, the star loves to bike, rock climb, and lift weights with her beau.

The former child star wrapped filming Parenthood this year after six seasons, playing Julia Braverman in the beloved NBC series. Up next, she’ll play opposite Gossip Girl’s Ed Westwick in the new ABC series Wicked City.
